Welcome to Campus Clash

Duke it out with your fellow classmates and find out whos the top College at UCSD!

Description at a glance

UCSD is made of 6 colleges: Sixth, Warren, Marshall, ERC, Muir, and Revelle. All of which are home to hundreds of league champions! Come join your fellow classmates in working together to show League of Tritons who is the number one house- I mean college - *cough cough* at UCSD!

How to play

There are several many ways to play! Anyone can earn points for their respective schools by being proactive!

To Play For Free: Coming out to events and signing in will automatically earn you a point along with any blitz points earned at any event

*OPTIONAL* Membership($10): Membership cards are bought from Triton Gaming and upon purchase gives your college a point!
